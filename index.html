Welcome to Probability Toss!

This website lets you experience probability in action through simple coin toss simulations.

🎯 Why this example?

Coin tosses offer a clean 50-50 chance and are perfect to demonstrate randomness and probability.

📊 What to expect?

Choose 1 to 4 coins to toss. After each set, you’ll see how often you get 1 head, 2 heads, etc.

📈 Crowd-Sourced Probabilities

As more people toss coins, the site updates live probabilities and compares them to theoretical values!
